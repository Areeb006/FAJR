About Fajr

Fajr Perfume Company was founded in 2019 by two close friends with a shared passion for fine fragrances and a vision to create a distinctive presence in the perfume industry. What began as a humble initiative quickly grew into a successful brand, thanks to their dedication to quality, innovation, and customer satisfaction.

Within a short span of time, Fajr Perfume successfully launched its products across 10 Indian states and 3 Union Territories, earning a loyal customer base and strong market presence. Building on this domestic success, the company proudly expanded its reach internationally and now exports to the United Arab Emirates (UAE), bringing the essence ofindian craflsmanship to a global audience. Fajr Perfume stands as a testament to entrepreneurial spirit, commitment to excellence, and the power of dreams turned into reality.
